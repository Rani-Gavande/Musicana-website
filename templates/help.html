Simply click on any song thumbnail, and it will start playing in the music player on the right side of your screen.

How can I upgrade to Premium?

Go to the Premium page and select your preferred subscription plan.

Can I download songs?

Yes, Premium users can download songs for offline listening by clicking the download icon.
